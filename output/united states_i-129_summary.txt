IMMIGRATION FORM SUMMARY
==================================================

Country: United States
Visa Category: Nonimmigrant Worker
Form Name: Petition for a Nonimmigrant Worker
Form ID: I-129
Governing Authority: U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS)

DESCRIPTION:
This form is used to petition for a nonimmigrant worker to come to the United States temporarily.

TARGET APPLICANTS:
Individuals or organizations seeking to employ nonimmigrant workers in the U.S.

SUBMISSION METHOD:
Submit the completed form to USCIS by mail or online, depending on the specific instructions provided.

PROCESSING TIME:
Varies based on the service center and the specific circumstances of the petition.

FEES:
Check the USCIS website for the current fee schedule for Form I-129.

SUPPORTING DOCUMENTS:
- Valid passport for each beneficiary
- Evidence of the employer's ability to pay the offered wage
- Labor Condition Application (if applicable)
- Any previous petitions or applications related to the beneficiary
