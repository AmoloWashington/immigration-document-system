IMMIGRATION FORM SUMMARY
==================================================

Country: United States
Visa Category: Nonimmigrant Worker
Form Name: Petition for Nonimmigrant Worker
Form ID: I-129MISC
Governing Authority: U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS)

DESCRIPTION:
This form is used by an employer to petition USCIS for an alien beneficiary to come temporarily to the United States as a nonimmigrant to perform services or labor, or to receive training.

TARGET APPLICANTS:
U.S. employers, U.S. agents, or U.S. sponsors filing for nonimmigrant classifications.

SUBMISSION METHOD:
Submit the completed form and supporting documents to USCIS by mail.

PROCESSING TIME:
None

FEES:
Filing fee as specified in the instructions.

SUPPORTING DOCUMENTS:
- Evidence required by statute and regulations
- Legible photocopies of required documents
- Full English translation of any foreign language documents
